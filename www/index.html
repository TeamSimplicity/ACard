ABOUT

The Alumni A-Card, which is free for all UBC alumni, gives you access to discounts from several partners, as well as a host of UBC services and venues. It also gives you access to online journals through EBSCO Academic and Business Source Alumni Editions. You can access these journals at www.alumni.ubc.ca/journals

If you want to use the card for community borrower privileges at the UBC Library, you must obtain a physical A-Card. You can pick up a physical card in the following ways:

  • from Alumni UBC at Cecil Green Park House
  • from the UBC Welcome Centre at Brock Hall (Room 1200)
  • from the UBC Robson Square front desk
  • from UBC Okanagan (Fipke Building 306)
  • from the UBC Okanagan Library
  • from the Hong Kong Regional Office
  • if you live outside of the above mentioned areas, we can mail the card to you. Contact: alumni.ubc@ubc.ca
